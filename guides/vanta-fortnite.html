Vanta Fortnite Setup Guide

Requirements

  • USB Thumb Drive
  • Your License Key (which should be YOURKEY)
  • Medal

1. Assigning Process

After purchasing Vanta Fortnite, you will be sent an email containing your license key. This key must be assigned to you accordingly.
  1. Contact our Telegram Bot (https://t.me/vantaesportsbot)
  2. Do /assign YOURKEY in the bots DMs.
  3. Once done, your License Key is assigned to your Telegram Account meaning that you successfully secured it. The bot will send you a Customer Server invite. Join to stay updated about status updates.

2. Installation Process

Once your License Key has been assigned to your Telegram Account you are ready to install the cheat.
  1. Do /getloader YOURKEY in the bots DMs.
  2. Once done, please patiently wait for your loader. This can take 5-20 minutes depending on the demand.

    Important side note: Your loader is bound to your Telegram Account meaning we wont have a hard time finding you in case you plan to share the loader with someone or if you have bad intentions in general. Just dont be a bad guy :3

  3. After receiving your loader download it and put it on your USB Thumb Drive for added securiy.

3. How to run Vanta

After installing Vanta properly you may want to proceed by running Vanta.
  1. Please close all the programs below (Use Task Manager).
    • Fortnite
    • Opera Browser
    • OBS Studio
    • Wallpaper Engine
  2. After closing the programs run the cheat loader as an administrator. To run a program as an administrator on Windows, right-click the program's icon and select "Run as administrator" from the context menu.
  3. You will be asked for a 2FA security code that has been sent to your Telegram DMs via our Telegram Bot (https://t.me/vantaesportsbot)

    Side note: This 2FA system has been introduced in Jan. 2024 as a precaution against keysharing and license key stealing. You will also be able to see every IP address that tries logging into your Vanta Account.

  4. In the Cheat Mode Selection screen you can finally open Fortnite.
  5. Once Fortnite has been opened you may proceed by selecting the mode of your choice.

Normal Mode

You will be asked to freshly open Medal in order to successfully activate the overlay.

Closet Mode

In closet mode, you will be prompted to enter your aimbot keybind, triggerbot keybind (if applicable) (You can find the keybind codes by clicking here). Additionally, you will have the option to activate fast reload and triggerbot, which are exclusively available to lifetime users.

Detection Test Mode

If you opt for "Detection Test Mode," you become a participant in testing the cheat detection capabilities. This includes assessing the detection of memory reads and overlays. Should you face a ban, please inform the developer https://t.me/sinceycheats.
